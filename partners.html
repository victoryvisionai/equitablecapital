What We Do

Three capabilities.
One relationship.

Every engagement starts with understanding the outcome you need. Then we deploy the GT Securities platform behind you to deliver it. No spreading thin, no handoffs between siloed advisors.

01
🏛
Capital Raising

Through GT Securities' institutional channels, we raise LP capital for emerging-manager funds (Fund I–III, sub-$500M), arrange direct lending for operating businesses and portfolio companies ($250K–$25M+ tickets), and introduce growth-equity capital to scaling founders.

Best Fit GPs raising a vehicle, RE operators with active deal pipelines, founder-CEOs preparing for institutional rounds.
02
🔄
M&A Advisory

Sellside representation for founder-owned and PE-backed companies ($10M–$500M EV). Buyside search and execution for strategic and financial acquirers. Recapitalizations and minority sales for founders seeking partial liquidity without losing control. Backed by GT Securities' $50B+ platform track record.

Best Fit Founders considering an exit, PE-backed companies preparing for transaction, strategic acquirers with defined M&A theses.
03
🤝
Strategic Introductions

Joint-venture partners, customers, channel relationships, and board or operating-advisor placements through Andrew's curated personal network. Not a standalone service line — this is how we deepen relationships before formal engagements arise.

Best Fit Founders building new business lines, GPs seeking portfolio-company growth levers, operators looking for capital partners or strategic LPs.
The Platform

Why the GT Securities
platform matters.

GT Securities is a registered broker-dealer and member of FINRA and SIPC. The platform combines the operational infrastructure of a full-service broker-dealer with the senior attention of a boutique firm. How We Work

Outcomes-first.
Relationship-led.

Every engagement begins with a 30-minute conversation. No documentation, no fees, no commitment. We figure out fit before either side commits to paperwork.

Step 01
Initial Conversation

30 minutes by phone or video. We listen to your situation, understand what a meaningful outcome looks like, and identify which of our capabilities — if any — would be relevant.

Step 02
Mutual Fit Assessment

If there is a fit, we propose a structure — placement, M&A advisory, or hybrid — and walk through the GT Securities engagement letter. Terms and fees are disclosed in writing before any retainer.
