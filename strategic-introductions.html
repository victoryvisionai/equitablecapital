The Problem

Most firms win clients
by accident.

Founder-led firms — boutiques, advisors, asset managers, fund GPs — usually grow on a mix of luck, referrals from a small circle, and conferences. It works until it doesn't. Then revenue depends on a handful of relationships, growth stalls, and there's no system to restart it.

The AI Client & Partner Development Desk replaces that fragility with a disciplined operating cadence: a defined target universe, a sequenced introduction strategy, and weekly measurable progress.

The Desk

Four functions.
One senior operator.

The AI Client & Partner Development Desk is a managed function for founder-led firms — combining senior judgment, AI-enabled relationship intelligence, and the disciplined operating cadence most firms know they need but can't build internally.

01
🗺
Target Universe Mapping

We define the specific clients, referral partners, and strategic relationships worth pursuing — with named individuals, ranked by fit and warmth, mapped to your network's existing pathways in.

Output A living target list of 100–500 named people with relationship scoring, intent signals, and intro pathways.

🤝
Introduction Strategy & Execution

We sequence and execute the introductions — warm where possible, professionally direct where not. Every touch is logged, every response tracked, every follow-up scheduled.

Output Weekly outreach cadence with sequenced touches across email, LinkedIn, phone, and in-person events.

⚙
AI Relationship Intelligence

Victory Vision powers the desk: AI scoring of contacts and intent signals, automated journey logging, content drafting tuned to each prospect, and real-time pipeline analytics.

Output Continuous prioritization of who to call this week and what to say — based on actual behavior data, not assumptions.

📊
Operating Cadence & Review

Weekly 60-minute review with you. What moved, what didn't, what's on deck, where capital and attention should go. No surprises. No black-box agencies. You see the desk every week.

How It Works

From engagement to
operating cadence in 30 days.

A defined 30-day stand-up, then a steady-state operating relationship measured by pipeline movement, not activity volume.

Week 01
Discovery & Scope

Define what a great client, partner, and strategic relationship looks like for your firm. Audit existing network and pipeline. Confirm the goal.

Week 02
Target Universe Build

Build the named target list, scored and ranked. Map your existing network's pathways into each target. Confirm the first 50 priority outreach names.

Week 03
Cadence Launch

Begin sequenced outreach. AI-enabled content drafting and intent scoring active. First introductions and meetings booked.

Wk 4+
Operating Cadence

Weekly review. Pipeline movement tracked and reported. Target universe refreshed quarterly. Cadence tuned to what's converting.
